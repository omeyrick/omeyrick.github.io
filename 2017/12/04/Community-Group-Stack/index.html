Community Group Operations Stack

A Basic Operations Stack For A Local Community Group

I’ve been working as the treasurer to a local community group here in Wandsworth, Playhouse Playgroup, and have been putting together a basic operations stack for them. I thought I’d share so that others can also use. It’s surprisingly cheap and surprisingly easy to automate a lot of the tasks that currently are either done manually or using a spreadsheet.

Here’s my list:

  • Email & Docs: G-Suite have a non-profit version that is free to use and comes with a bunch of additional extras (e.g. Adwords credits) should you be interested in making use of these. We make regular use of Google Docs and Sheets to share information, and as some of the information we are storing is quite sensitive, it makes sense to use a corporate-style account for this.

  • Website: We just built our own from a free static template sourced from Codrops. There are so many of these types of template available these days, and as long as you only need a static site then it’s fairly simple to put together with even basic html skills. Hosting is on fasthosts.

  • Accounts: Xero. With a non-profit discount this becomes a good option. Easy for our accountant to use, automatic bank feeds, and reconciliation is a breeze.

  • Payments: Stripe. Initially I wasn’t keen as I was worried about the cost, but they also have non-profit pricing (1.2% on EU cards). Sign up is simple, and it integrates well with Paperform

  • Forms: Paperform. I was looking for a really simple way to send out a registration form, ideally with payment processing included. A club management system is too much for our needs, and too expensive anyway. Paperform is cheap, integrates with Stripe and Paypal, connects to Google Sheets as well, and most important of all, very easy to use. Great product.

  • Automation: Zapier. Connecting all these services so the whole process runs easily was a key consideration for me as at some point someone else will need to take over and run the various finance and admin processes. Zapier have a free basic service and integrations with Google Sheets, Paperform, Stripe & Xero (although the Xero integration is paid).

  • Social: Facebook of course

Next up is adding in donations and ways to collect Gift Aid.
