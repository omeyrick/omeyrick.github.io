Insomnia

I blame it on the kids. Three children in three years and I just feel permanently jet-lagged. To be fair to them, the children don’t wake up anymore, but I do. 2am on the dot every night.

On a good night I roll over and go to sleep. Most nights I just lie there waiting to go back to sleep, which as any insomniac can tell you, is not a very good way to go back to sleep.

I’ve tried counting sheep, podcasts, cutting out all blue-light, breathing exercises, getting up and reading, getting up and coding (not a good solution). Latterly my lovely wife has persuaded me to throw money at the problem with the Sleep School. Lets see.

It’s a horrible thing insomnia. You lie awake at night with growing frustration, knowing that the next day you’ll be a wreck. You feel useless. Nothing gets done.

It’s such a simple thing, and there are obviously many worse things that can happen. One day I’ll sort it out, I just need a good nights sleep first.
