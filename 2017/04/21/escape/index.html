Project Escape

Places to live in the UK

A few months back I started building a map of the UK, overlaying things like transport links, schools, nice places to live, to help me figure out where my family and I could end up living one day. London’s great… but not forever.

There are still a bunch of rough edges I’d like to smooth off:

  • The colours are awful
  • I want to improve the text hover to actually contain useful information
  • There’s quite a lot of data behind this which I’d like to expose somehow

Here’s the plot. To unclutter everything click on the legend to hide / reveal different datasets. This is best viewed on a bigger screen, otherwise you will get redirected to the Plotly backend when you click on the map.
