About Me

As a VC once kindly said to me: my career has been a bit of a “hodgepodge”. I started out as a consultant and have ended up as an entrepreneur, taking time to work in private equity and the non-profit sector in between. I’ve lived and worked in over 10 countries, including time in the Middle East, South America and Asia. I’ve had a lot of fun along the way, and made some great friends.

I’m now back living in the UK with my family, still involved with my company DemystData, working with a few other startups, still learning (how to code most recently thanks to MITx).
